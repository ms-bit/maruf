About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech, where I am being advised by Anuj Karpatne. I completed my undergradute in Computer Science and Engineering from Department of CSE, BUET, Bangladesh.

My current research focuses on Machine Learning & Data Mining. In particular, I am studying the graph neural networks and trying to make it more resilient to propagate physical knowledge through it. My goal is to make machine learning algorithms more consistent with the physical rules. I have previously worked on projects on computational biology.
